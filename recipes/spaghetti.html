Spaghetti

Homemade spaghetti with ground beef and peppers

This is a simple recipe to cook homemade spaghetti using spaghetti noodles, spaghetti sauce, ground beef, onions and peppers

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Red pepper
  • Yellow pepper
  • Orange pepper
  • Italian seasoning blend
  • Garlic powder

Steps:

  1. Boil water
  2. Start cooking beef
  3. Chop onion and peppers
  4. Add noodles to water
  5. Add vegetables to beef
  6. Heat up spaghetti sauce
  7. Add garlic powder and italian seasoning blend to beef and sauce
  8. Drain water for noodles
  9. Add beef and vegetables to sauce
  10. Serve noodles with sauce on top
